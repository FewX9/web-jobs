Job Highlights

Job Description

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Collaborate with engineering and product teams to help build features and solve creative problems
  • Create and set up complex prefabs in Unity
  • Create real-time effects
  • Set up environment and lighting
  • Iterate on Unity 3D scene implementation to finalize product and other creative requirements
  • Creating performant graphics shaders for environments and effects
  • Support communication and asset pipeline between engineering and artist teams

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Experience with Unity Game Engine
  • Experience in scripting and debugging with C# within the Unity environment (knowledge of other scripting languages, such as Python, C++, JavaScript, Mel is a plus)
  • Experience in shader creation (Cg shading language)
  • Experience with Environment/Lighting setup
  • Experience creating real-time visual effects sequences
  • Good knowledge in optimization for mobile platforms
  • Basic knowledge in Git-like repositories
  • To apply for a senior level, 3+ years of experience and expert level in C#, Unity, Shaders, VFX, Maya or other 3D packages are required.
